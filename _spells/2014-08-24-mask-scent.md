Druid, Ranger
_1st-level Transmutation_

**Casting:** 1 action (V, S)

**Range:** Touch

**Area of Effect:** 1 creature

**Duration:** 1 hour

You mask the scent of one creature. The target can not be perceived through Blindsight (smell).
